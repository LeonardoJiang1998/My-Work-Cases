THE INTRODUCTION
Metaverse Development is a website trying to be the encyclopedia of the metaverse which aims to explain the idea of metaverse to people and focus on the development of metaverse. The website is divided into five parts: what it is, how it works, where it goes, latest news and forum. So, you might find they are not fully functional.

What it is will tell you the definition of metaverse.

How it works will tell you how you can live in metaverse.

Where it goes will tell you the possible future of metaverse.

Latest news is a place you can find the latest news about metaverse.

Forum can help you find others who are concerned about metaverse.

For now, the latest news and forum parts are still under construction.

The understanding of the metaverse at this website is generally based on the understanding of the builder of this website. If you have any questions or misunderstandings, welcome to email the builder.

LEADING TECH COMPANIES IN METAVERSE DEVELOPMENT
Meta, a company has a social media empire and the most popular VR headset the Oculus Quest 2. Changing its name from Facebook to Meta in 2021 shows its determination to developing metaverse related software and hardware. It might make the connection in the metaverse possible. Its founder produced a video explaining what the metaverse means to them. You can find it here.
Mark Zuckerberg's Video about Meta
Nvidia is one of the most advanced chipset design and computing tech companies. From the gaming GPUs in mass consuming markets to the professional computing chips in the supercomputer, they all come from Nvidia. It also provides the chips and systems used in self-driving vehicles. It might become the physical foundation of the metaverse world.
Neuralink does research on the brain-machine interface. As one of the frontier technology explorers, it reveals the infinite possibilities of the metaverse future. It could be very helpful in assisting people with disabilities. Elon Musk as its founder also pursuits exploring Mars and self-driving vehicles. Combing them all together, a piece of the blueprint of the metaverse the world might be more clear.
